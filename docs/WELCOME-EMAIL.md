# Welcome email / DM templates — influencer beta wave

Drafts in three lengths so you can pick by channel: IG/X/TikTok DM
(tight), email (longer), and follow-up (gentle nudge after 7 days).
Replace `{{...}}` placeholders before sending.

## Variables
- `{{name}}` — first name only, never their full handle
- `{{platform}}` — their primary platform (Instagram / YouTube / TikTok / X)
- `{{their_thing}}` — one *specific* thing they did that you actually saw
  (e.g. "your euclidean drum video last month", "the BC-LAB1 patch reel")
- `{{code}}` — their unique beta code, e.g. `BETA-XOVND-OPEN-003`

## Tone notes

- Keep `joan@xovnd.com` as the reply-to. People reply to humans, not
  contact@.
- Don't ask for an affiliate commitment or a paid post in the first
  touch. Build the relationship.
- The "no offence taken" phrasing is the single most-replied-to line
  in this category of outreach in 2026. Keep it.
- If an influencer is in a non-English-speaking market, translate the
  whole thing — Google Translate is fine for a first DM.
- Do *not* send the same wording to 10 people in a row in the same
  platform — Instagram's spam detector flags identical DMs.

## Quick send checklist before each DM
- [ ] Their name spelt right?
- [ ] `{{their_thing}}` is real and specific (not "I love your stuff")?
- [ ] Code matches the spreadsheet row?
- [ ] Logged the send date in the spreadsheet?
